# Privacy Policy for MiniCPM-V Demo

**Last updated: April 2026**

## Overview

MiniCPM-V Demo ("the App") is an on-device multimodal large language model
demonstration application. The App performs all AI inference locally on your
device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** The App does not contain any analytics
SDK, advertising SDK, or third-party data collection tool.

## Data Processing

All image, audio, and text inputs you provide are processed entirely on your
device. None of this data is transmitted to our servers or any third party.

## Required Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions, all used strictly for
local functionality:

- **Camera**: to capture images for on-device analysis.
- **Photo Library**: to select images for on-device analysis.
- **Microphone**: to record audio for on-device speech recognition.
- **Speech Recognition**: to convert speech to text locally.
- **Local Network**: to download model weights from public model repositories
  (e.g., Hugging Face, ModelScope). No personal data is transmitted.

You may revoke any of these permissions at any time in iOS Settings. The App
may have reduced functionality without them.

## Model Downloads

The App downloads model weight files from publicly accessible URLs such as
Hugging Face and ModelScope. Downloading these files follows the privacy
practices of those services. We do not log or record which models you download.

## Data Storage

All model files and user-generated content remain in the App's sandboxed
Documents directory on your device. Uninstalling the App removes all local data.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be published
on this page with an updated date.
